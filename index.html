TOURNAMENT INSTRUCTIONS

Tournament Details

Important information about the tournament schedule and venue:

  • Date: 17th October 2025
  • Check-in Time: 2:30 PM (Please arrive 30 minutes early)
  • Venue: Avantika Food Garden, 2nd Floor, Bazar Road, Jamatra
  • Entry Fee:50/- per player which is equals to 200/- per team (Submit at venue)

What to Bring

Essential items every participant must have:

  • Your mobile device with Free Fire MAX installed
  • Device charger and power bank
  • Headphones for better communication
  • Valid ID proof for verification
  • Entry fee of 50/- per player which is equals to 200/- per team (Submit at venue)

Game Preparation

Get your game and device ready for the tournament:

  • Make sure your device is fully charged
  • Update Free Fire MAX to the latest version
  • Clear cache and close background apps
  • Test your internet connection beforehand
  • Have your game account ready and updated

Team Requirements

Important guidelines for team participation:

  • Each team must have exactly 4 players
  • All players must be present at the venue
  • Team name should be appropriate and respectful
  • One registration per player across all teams
  • No player substitutions after registration

Rules & Regulations

Tournament rules that all participants must follow:

  • Follow all instructions from tournament organizers
  • Maintain sportsmanship throughout the event
  • No abusive language or behavior
  • Decisions by organizers are final
  • Any disputes must be raised immediately

Prize Distribution

Information about prizes and winners:

  • Prize money will be distributed to the winning team
  • Distribution will happen after the final match
  • All team members must be present to receive prizes
  • Prizes are subject to applicable taxes
  • Organizers reserve the right to modify prize structure
WARNING: Any suspicious activity including using panels, hacks, or any unauthorized software will result in immediate disqualification and permanent ban from all future PhenixMax tournaments. Fair play is our top priority!
